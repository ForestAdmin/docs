title: Inbox
description: Automatically distribute and process tasks across your team with Inboxes.
---

The **Inbox** feature automatically assigns tasks to team members based on workload and dispatch rules, improving efficiency and ensuring no record falls through the cracks.

An inbox is always tied to a **segment of a collection** — for example, the `Waiting for Validation` segment of a `Documents` collection. Every record in that segment becomes a task for operators to process.

<Info>
The Inbox feature must be enabled for your project. Contact your Forest Admin account manager to activate it.
</Info>

## Creating an Inbox

1. Navigate to the **Collaboration** tab.
2. Enable **Layout Editor** mode.
3. In the left menu, find the **Inboxes** section and click **Create Inbox**.
4. Select the **collection** and **segment** that defines the tasks.
5. Choose a **dispatch rule**:

| Dispatch rule | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Field-based** | Records are sorted by up to 3 fields (ascending or descending). For example: sort by creation date descending, then by priority ascending. |
| **Random** | Records are assigned in random order. |

## Processing Tasks

Once an inbox is created, operators can access it from the **Collaboration** tab.

### Todo

The **Todo** count shows the number of unassigned records in the segment. Click **Start processing** to be automatically assigned to the next task and redirected to the record.

After completing the work on that record (e.g. triggering a `Validate Document` action), click **Next ticket** in the top bar to be assigned a new task automatically.

### Doing

The **Doing** section shows tasks currently assigned to you. You can navigate back to an in-progress record from here.

### Backlog

The **Backlog** is visible to operators with a **Manager, Editor, Developer, or Administrator** permission level. It provides an overview of all records in the segment:

- Which tasks are unassigned, in progress, or completed.
- Which operator is assigned to each task.
- For in-progress tasks: when the task was started.
- For completed tasks: the total handling time (including approval time if applicable).

## Manual Task Assignment

Managers can manually assign a specific record to an operator from:

- The **Backlog** section of the inbox.
- The **record detail view**, using the native **"Assign to..."** action — which also lets you assign records to operators outside your current team.

Manual assignments bypass the sorting rules and become the next task for that operator.

## Inbox Settings

Access inbox settings from **Layout Editor mode** → the menu next to the inbox name. You can:

- **Rename** or **delete** the inbox.
- **Organize inboxes into folders** for cleaner navigation.
- **Set a maximum number of concurrent tasks per operator** — when reached, clicking "Next ticket" redirects the operator to one of their in-progress tasks instead of assigning a new one.
- **Automatically unassign tasks** after a configurable idle time, making them available to other operators.

<Info>
Maximum concurrent tasks and automatic unassignment are disabled by default.
</Info>
