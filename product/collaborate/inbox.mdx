---
title: Inbox
---

The Inbox feature efficiently distributes tasks among team members based on their workload and expertise, improving the efficiency of your workflows.

## Creating an Inbox

To create an inbox in Forest Admin, navigate to the Collaboration tab and enable the Layout Editor mode. In the left navbar, you'll find an "Inboxes" section where you can create a new one.

An inbox is always based on a segment of a collection. For example, you can create an inbox based on the `Documents` collection and the `Waiting for Validation` segment, which contains all the documents that have not been verified yet.

## Processing tickets

After creation, click on "Start processing" to automatically allocate it to the next task — in this example, verifying a document.

You will then be redirected to the record view where you can verify the document and update the relevant fields.

At the top, a bar will appear indicating that you are currently processing a task from the inbox. By clicking on "Next ticket", you will be automatically assigned a new task, helping you complete tasks efficiently.
