title: Export Data

Forest Admin lets you export collection data to **CSV** directly from the UI. Exports respect your active filters and segments, so you can export exactly the records you need.

## Exporting Records

1. Open the collection you want to export.
2. Optionally, apply **filters** or switch to a **segment** to narrow the records.
3. Click the **Export** button (download icon) in the top-right toolbar.
4. Forest Admin will generate and download a CSV file with the current record set.

<Info>
The export includes all records matching your current filter — not just the records on the current page. Pagination does not affect exports.
</Info>

## What Gets Exported

The CSV export includes:

- All **visible columns** in the current table view (as configured in the Layout Editor).
- All records matching the **active filter and segment**.
- Field values formatted as plain text.

Relationships (linked records) are exported as their identifier or display field, depending on your configuration.

## Export Limits

For very large datasets, exports may take a moment to generate. Forest Admin processes the export in the background and downloads the file when ready.

<Warning>
Extremely large exports (hundreds of thousands of records) may be slow. Consider applying filters to reduce the dataset before exporting.
</Warning>

## Exporting Approval Request History

From the **Approval Workflows** tab, you can also export the history of approval requests over a given period. Click the **Export** button in that tab and select the desired time interval — you'll receive a CSV file by email.

## Best Practices

- **Filter before exporting** — apply segments or filters to export only the relevant data.
- **Check column visibility** — only visible columns are exported; configure your table view in the [Layout Editor](/product/build/layout-editor) before exporting.
- **Handle sensitive data carefully** — exports may contain personal or confidential information. Ensure exports are handled according to your data governance policies.
