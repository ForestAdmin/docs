---
title: Dashboards
---

A Dashboard serves as a centralized space for visualizing and analyzing key data metrics and insights. Create various types of charts, including time-based charts, leaderboards, value charts, pie charts, etc., based on all your structured data plugged into Forest Admin. This structured data setup allows for complex filters and advanced data visualization.

For maximum flexibility, code the HTML/CSS/JS of your charts, enabling advanced data visualization tailored to your specific needs. The computation of data runs through an HTTP URL, ensuring that you don't encounter limitations and integrating dynamic data into your dashboard. Dashboards in Forest Admin let you efficiently monitor and derive valuable insights from your structured data, facilitating informed decision-making and optimal data management within your admin panel.
