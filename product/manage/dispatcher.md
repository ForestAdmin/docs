# Dispatcher

The Dispatcher feature in Forest Admin helps you distribute work efficiently across your team by automatically assigning tasks and records to team members.

## Overview

Dispatcher allows you to:
- Automatically assign records to team members based on rules
- Balance workload across your team
- Track assignment history
- Set up round-robin or rule-based assignment strategies

## How it works

The Dispatcher integrates with the **Assign to** action to automatically route records to the right team member based on your defined rules.

* [Assign to Action](/product/process/actions/overview.md) - Learn more about the Assign to action

## Configuration

Dispatcher configuration is available in your project settings. You can define:
- Assignment rules based on record properties
- Team member availability
- Workload balancing strategies
- Priority handling

## Use cases

- **Customer support**: Automatically assign support tickets to available agents
- **Sales**: Distribute leads evenly across sales representatives
- **Operations**: Route orders to fulfillment teams based on location
- **Content moderation**: Balance content review tasks across moderators

{% hint style="info" %}

The Dispatcher feature is part of Forest Admin's collaboration tools. Contact your account manager to enable this feature.

{% endhint %}
