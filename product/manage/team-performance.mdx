This page is a work in progress. Team Performance metrics are part of an upcoming feature set. Check back for updates, or contact your Forest Admin account manager for the latest information.

The **Team Performance** section will provide managers and team leads with aggregated metrics and analytics about how their team is performing — including task throughput, response times, and workload distribution.

In the meantime, you can track team activity using:

- **[Activity Logs](/product/manage/activity)** — a complete audit trail of all actions and events.
- **[Inbox](/product/collaborate/inbox)** — the Backlog section shows task assignment and completion data per operator.
- **[Charts](/product/manage/charts/overview)** — build custom dashboards using your operational data.
