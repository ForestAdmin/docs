title: "Zendesk app"
description: "Embed Forest Admin data directly inside Zendesk tickets and customer profiles."
---

The Forest Admin Zendesk app lets you view and act on your data without leaving Zendesk. Access customer records, trigger actions, and see related data inline in ticket sidebars.

<Note>This feature may not be available on all plans. Check the pricing page for more information, or contact us to enable it.</Note>

## How it works

The app uses Forest Admin's embed capabilities to surface your collections inside Zendesk. Install the app from the Zendesk marketplace, connect it to your Forest Admin project, and configure which collections to display.

## Setup

1. Install the Forest Admin app from the Zendesk marketplace.
2. Connect the app to your Forest Admin project using your project credentials.
3. Configure which collections to display in ticket sidebars and customer profiles.
