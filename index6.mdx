# One operating model. Every actor.

<Columns cols={2}>
  Regulated operations are splintered across tools, teams, and now AI agents, each with its own access, its own logic, its own blind spots.

  Forest collapses that into one operating model. The same data, the same workflows, the same audit trail, no matter who or what is executing.
</Columns>

### Three things that stay true, whoever acts

<Columns cols={3}>
  <div>
    **One data model**

    Your databases and services, unified, no copies, no migration. Data stays in your infrastructure.
  </div>
  <div>
    **One set of rules**

    Permissions and business logic apply equally to people, partners, and AI agents.
  </div>
  <div>
    **One audit trail**

    Every action records who or what executed, what changed, and why. Audit-ready, always.
  </div>
</Columns>

<Info>
  Forest hosts the UI and configuration. Your data never leaves your network. [See the architecture.](/get-started/connect/architectures/self-hosted)
</Info>
