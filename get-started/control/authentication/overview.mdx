## Standard login

Users log in to Forest with their email and password at [app.forestadmin.com](https://app.forestadmin.com).

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

Forest supports SAML 2.0 SSO, allowing your users to log in via your Identity Provider (IdP). SSO is configured at the **Organization** level.

<Info>
  SSO requires the [Forest Enterprise plan](https://www.forestadmin.com/pricing). At least one project in the organization must be on this plan.
</Info>

Supported providers with step-by-step guides:

- [Google Workspace](/get-started/control/authentication/sso-providers/google)
- [Okta](/get-started/control/authentication/sso-providers/okta)
- [Azure Active Directory](/get-started/control/authentication/sso-providers/azure)
- [AWS IAM Identity Center](/get-started/control/authentication/sso-providers/aws)
- [Generic SAML 2.0](/get-started/control/authentication/sso-providers/generic-saml)

### How users log in with SSO

On the Forest login page, click **"Login with SSO"**, enter the name of your Organization, and click **"Login"**.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

2FA is available to all users. You can also **require** 2FA before accessing specific environments from **Project Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication**.

See [2FA documentation](/get-started/control/authentication/2fa).

## SCIM provisioning

Manage users at scale through your Identity Provider using SCIM. This lets you add users to Forest projects directly from your IdP, with the correct role and permissions.

Supported providers: Okta and OneLogin.

See [SCIM documentation](/get-started/control/authentication/scim-okta).

## Security features

Additional security features are available in **Project Settings → Security**:

- **IP whitelisting** — Restrict access to specific IP addresses
- **Auto logout** — Force session expiry after inactivity
- **2FA enforcement** — Require 2FA for specific environments
