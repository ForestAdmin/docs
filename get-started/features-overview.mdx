Forest is built on four foundations. Each one solves a problem regulated teams hit when their operations grow beyond what spreadsheets, generic admin panels, or one-off scripts can support.

## Sovereign Data backbone

Your operational data layer, live, writable, and never leaves your infrastructure.

- Act on live data from every source, in one place.
- Live read and write straight to your databases, no ETL project, no pipeline to maintain.
- Schema changes never break Forest: it adapts automatically.
- Your data never leaves your infrastructure. Forest sees layout and metadata, not records.
- Drop in custom logic when a workflow needs it.

</Card>

## Intelligent Ops Orchestration

Automate at the speed your regulator allows, with humans always in reach.

- Run AI agents on real workflows without losing human oversight.
- One workflow spans teams, partners, and BPOs.
- Pause for human review, approval, or escalation at any step.
- Move from manual to autonomous on your timeline, no engineering required.

<Card title="Set up workflows" icon="diagram-project" href="/get-started/set-up-operations">
  Turn manual playbooks into orchestrated workflows.
</Card>

## Compliance & AI Governance

Compliance built into every layer, for humans and AI agents alike.

- Prove every action (human or agent) at the record level.
- Control what every human and every agent can touch.
- Defend any AI decision in front of a regulator with the full reasoning log.
- Built with hundreds of regulated teams: patterns you don't have to invent.

<Card title="Govern access" icon="shield-halved" href="/get-started/control/roles-permissions">
  Roles, permissions, audit trails, SSO, and SCIM.
</Card>

## Composable & Headless

Every Forest capability available wherever your teams, partners, and agents work.

- Claude and other AI agents act on your operations safely, Forest enforces permissions and writes the audit trail.
- Bring Forest data and actions into Zendesk, n8n, Claude, or any tool your team already uses.
- One operational graph: a single source of truth for what happened, regardless of where the action started.
