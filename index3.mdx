---
title: "From zero to running operations"
description: "Five steps from a fresh connection to a governed, AI-ready operation."
mode: "wide"
---

You don't have to read the whole manual to get value out of Forest. Follow these steps in order, each one builds on the last, and you'll have a real operation running on your own data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your data">
    Point Forest at your existing databases and services, SQL, MongoDB, Stripe, HubSpot, and more. One data model, no migration, no copying data out.

    <Card title="Connect a data source" icon="plug" href="/get-started/connect/data-sources/overview">
      Browse every supported source and connect your first one.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shape the back-office">
    Build the views your team actually works from. Filter, group, and lay out records around the job to be done, not the schema.

    <Card title="Customize your back-office" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/get-started/customize-your-back-office">
      Turn raw tables into a usable operations UI.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your business logic">
    Computed fields, validation, custom actions, and hooks, written in Node.js or Ruby. The rules live in code you own.

    <Card title="Add business logic" icon="code" href="/get-started/add-business-logic">
      Write your first action and computed field.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expose it to AI agents">
    Open your data and workflows to AI agents over MCP. They inherit the same permissions and leave the same audit trail as your team.

    <Card title="Expose to AI agents" icon="robot" href="/get-started/expose-to-ai-agents">
      Stand up an MCP server over your operation.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and invite your team">
    Ship it, then bring in operators, reviewers, and developers, each with the right permission level.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Deploy" icon="rocket" href="/get-started/deploy">
        Push your operation live.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Invite your team" icon="user-plus" href="/get-started/invite-your-team">
        Add people and set permissions.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  In a hurry? The [15-minute quickstart](/get-started/quickstart) collapses these steps into a single guided run.
</Note>
